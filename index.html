About CityCare

CityCare is a community-driven support hub created to help residents access essential resources, services, and assistance programs. Whether you need emergency help, community events, volunteer opportunities, or important city services—CityCare connects you.

Support Services

  • Emergency Help (shelters, food banks, hotlines)
  • Health & Wellness Services
  • Youth, Senior, and Family Programs
  • Local Government & Public Services
  • Volunteer & Donation Opportunities
  • Community Events and Initiatives

Community Impact

CityCare highlights neighborhood stories, featured volunteers, and the positive impact our programs have on families and individuals in our city.

We believe a connected community is a stronger community.
