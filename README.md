CityCare Connect
Project Overview

CityCare Connect is a community resource hub developed for the Technology Student Association (TSA) Webmaster competition. The website centralizes essential local resources and services into one accessible platform, helping community members easily find the support and information they need.

CityCare Connect demonstrates skills in web development, visual design, content research, and project management, while addressing a real-world community challenge: improving access to reliable and organized local resources.

Project Purpose

The purpose of CityCare Connect is to:

Serve as a centralized hub for community and city resources

Improve accessibility to essential services and information

Reduce confusion by organizing resources in one platform

Support community members through clear, reliable content

Target Audience

CityCare Connect is designed for:

Community members seeking local support services

Students and families

Educators and community organizations

Technology Student Association (TSA) judges

The site is built to be inclusive, easy to navigate, and accessible to users of varying technical skill levels.
